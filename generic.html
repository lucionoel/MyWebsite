I Worked in Carmela Coffee Company as a data analyst for 6 months. Over the time span agreed on, I was assigned a project which was automating the inventory process using sales data. I utilized sales data provided by Carmelasâ€™s POS company, which was Clover, and automized the inventory process by calculating the amount of product used per sale of item.
